# SignPD


SignPD – Your in-browser PDF workshop
Drop any PDF (or drag it) and instantly add signatures, text, highlights, drawings, sticky notes, stamps, measurements, images—even extra pages with lined, grid, checklist or meeting-note templates. Rearrange, rotate or delete pages with a quick thumbnail strip. Everything lives in your browser (no uploads, no backend, no account) and auto-saves as you work. When you’re done, hit Save & Download to get a flattened, ready-to-share PDF. Fast, private, and free.
